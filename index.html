Based in Atlanta, GA, Jay Alan is a filmmaker and photographer whose visual language is built on intimacy, practical light, and narrative tension. Every frame is a deliberate compositional choice — whether it's a studio portrait or a wide establishing shot in an open field.

His work spans music videos, short films, and long-form series. Credits include directing the short film Baby Girl (2024), the music video About Her for Bambii, and the ongoing Afro-fantasy epic Worship. His cinematographic approach favors low-key practical lighting, deep shadows, and the stillness that holds meaning.

Available for commercial, narrative, and editorial work.
